# Content and Bilingual QA

## Authoritative identity

**SAMT**  
**Strategic Advancement, Management & Transformation**  
**برنامج سَمْت للتقدم الاستراتيجي والإدارة والتحول**

Master idea:

**From Potential to Command**  
**من الإمكانات إلى القيادة**

## Core programme definition

SAMT is presented as an elite, highly selective leadership programme that identifies exceptional talent and prepares selected individuals for future command, strategic leadership and greater institutional responsibility.

The website does not describe SAMT as:

- an open-enrolment programme
- a conventional training course
- a standard academic qualification
- a protocol travel programme

## Preferred participant terminology

- English: SAMT Fellows
- Arabic: زملاء سَمْت or منتسبو سَمْت depending on institutional preference

## Bilingual method

- English and Arabic are authored separately rather than mechanically mirrored.
- Arabic switches the document to `dir="rtl"`.
- Display typography, navigation, lists, modal alignment and directional controls respond to the active direction.
- Arabic retains the approved word **برنامج**, not **منظومة**, in the formal descriptor.

## Programme arrangements disclaimer

Host categories, station activities and institutional references are illustrative. They are expressly stated as subject to formal coordination, approval and agreement.

## Grammar and consistency checks completed

- Consistent title case in English interface labels
- British spelling retained where appropriate: programme, judgement
- Consistent use of “Command Readiness”
- Consistent use of “Preparation Stations”
- Clear distinction between selection, assessment, formation, exposure and transformation
- No claim of confirmed external partnership
