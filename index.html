FROM POTENTIAL TO COMMAND

Programme overview

Selected for potential.
Shaped for command.

SAMT is a highly selective leadership programme for exceptional professionals who demonstrate the judgement, character, discipline and ambition required for future command and senior strategic responsibility.

The programme does not reward attendance. It evidences readiness.
