# SAMT Linguistic Validation Record

## Scope
This record distinguishes the established Arabic lexical meaning of **السَّمْت** from the SAMT programme's strategic interpretation of that meaning.

## Authoritative lexical basis
Primary source: **مجمع اللغة العربية بالقاهرة — المعجم الوسيط، مادة «السَّمْت»**.

Official entries:
- https://www.arabicacademy.gov.eg/ar/محرك-البحث/السَّمْتُ?exact_search=true
- https://www.arabicacademy.gov.eg/ar/search_engine/roots/سمت

The Academy defines **السَّمْت** through the following principal meanings:

- الطريق الواضح
- المذهب
- السكينة والوقار
- الهيئة

The related verb entry also supports meanings of intention and following a direction or manner, including **قَصَدَه** and **نحا نحوَه**.

## Approved content distinction
The Story pages must present:

1. **Lexical fact:** clear path, adopted approach or manner, serenity, dignity and bearing.
2. **Programme interpretation:** SAMT uses the language of path and purpose as a disciplined direction toward leadership responsibility.

The programme interpretation must not be represented as an additional dictionary definition.

## Correction completed
Phase 4 removes the earlier wording that treated “elevation / العلو” as a principal lexical meaning. Elevation and advancement remain part of the programme's strategic narrative, not the dictionary definition of the name.

## Controlled English wording
> In Arabic, al-samt is defined through interconnected meanings that include a clear path, an adopted approach or manner, serenity, dignity and bearing. The programme interprets this language of path and purpose as a disciplined direction toward leadership responsibility.

## Controlled Arabic wording
> تتمحور الدلالات المعجمية لكلمة السَّمْت حول الطريق الواضح والمذهب والسكينة والوقار والهيئة. ويستثمر البرنامج معنى الطريق والقصد بوصفه اتجاهاً منضبطاً نحو مسؤولية القيادة.

## Status
Validated for Phase 4 publication, subject to any later formal institutional linguistic approval process.
